Common Questions

Frequently asked questions.

A mortgage broker works on your behalf to shop and compare mortgage products from dozens of different lenders — banks, credit unions, and private lenders. Unlike going to a single bank, I find the best rate and terms for your specific situation. My services are free to you in most cases, as I'm compensated by the lender.

In most cases, my services are completely free to you. I'm paid by the lender when your mortgage funds. In rare situations involving private or alternative lending, there may be a broker fee — but I'll always be transparent about costs upfront before you commit to anything.

Getting pre-approved is easy! You can apply online or book a call with me. I'll review your income, debts, and credit to determine how much you can afford and lock in a rate for you — so you can shop with confidence.

Not necessarily! Your bank's renewal offer is usually not their best rate. I can shop around and often find significantly better terms. Many of my clients have saved thousands of dollars a year just by letting me negotiate their renewal instead of signing the letter their bank sent.

I'm based in Squamish and serve the entire Sea to Sky Corridor including Whistler, Pemberton, and the surrounding areas. I also work with clients across British Columbia. Much of the process can be done remotely, so don't hesitate to reach out no matter where you are!

A pre-approval can often be done within 24–48 hours. Once you've found a property and we submit a full application, approval typically takes 3–5 business days. I'll keep you updated every step of the way so there are no surprises.
